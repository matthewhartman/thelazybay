Parks / Beaches

Place Description
Marina Park Famous for ‘the ship’ with the zip-line. Playground, grassy picnic areas, BBQs, swimming beach, bathrooms.
San Buenaventura State Beach‬ Park BBQs, bathrooms, grassy picnic area, snack bar, volleyball nets, the beginning of paved Bike trail. Bike and beach equipment rentals.

Surf breaks/ Kite surfing beaches

Place Description
C-Street / Surfers Point right hand point break, suitable for surfing and kite surfing.
Emma wood beach right and left hand beach break. Suitable for surfing and kite surfing.
Rincon right and left hand point break – Suitable for experienced surfers.
Point Mugu right and left hand beach break – suitable for all surfers.

Bike Trails

Ojai paved bike path; starts and San Buenaventura Park and runs 22 miles to Ojai. Scenic path hugs the coastline and is suitable for the entire family. Pier and surfers point is 10 minutes along the path; from there you also head up California Street which will take you to Main Street.

Main Street

Check out Main Street which offers a chilled relaxed vibe, fine dining, casual dining, coffee shops, shopping, thrift shops/ recycled fashion, wine tasting, movie theater and more. Also has nightlife with live concerts and bars, dress up or keep it casual, just head out and see where the night takes you.
