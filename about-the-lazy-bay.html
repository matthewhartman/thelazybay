Welcome to Paradise! ____

Just a 2-minute stroll to the sand, this bright and colorful beach bungalow has everything you need for a perfect getaway! The large private, sun-filled backyard is fully equipped for maximum relaxation and fun; includes a 6 person spa, 2 person hammock, gas fire pit, weber gas BBQ, large dinning table with umbrella, oversized cushioned loveseat and chairs, shaded lounge hut and a huge chalkboard for the creative ones.

Also included in your paradise away from home; beach chairs, beach towels, ice chest (perfect for wheeling refreshments to the beach), board games, cards, puzzles and oversized top quality soft bath towels (no small & rough bath towels here!).

You’ll be charmed by the unique and cozy interior design of this funky beach bungalow. Kick up your feet and enjoy the wood burning stove, 47 inch TV with direct TV, DVD and wireless high speed internet. Enjoy a touch of luxury in the tastefully designed ‘captain’s quarters’ with a queen Temperpedic mattress and 1450 count Egyptian cotton sheets. We haven’t forgotten about the crew; the ‘crew’s quarters’ include; two twin Sealy Posturepedic mattresses and a nautical theme that will have you drift asleep dreaming of adventures on the open sea.

The brand new kitchen is fully equipped with everything you will need to prepare and enjoy meals. Meal area includes a 6-seat dining table; French doors leading out to the back patio and a beautiful wall mural.

And the biggest bonus of all, the location! Hang up the car keys, within 2 minutes walk you can dine at one of the many seaward restaurants & cafes, such as seaward fish n’ chips (arguably the world’s best) and ‘social tap’ a popular restaurant and bar with outdoor seating, live music & big screen TVs. Also within the 5 minute walk there’s Vons supermarket, Starbucks, Mc Donald’s, subway, in & out, Wienerschnitzel, Carrow’s, liquor store, yogurt and smoothie shop and much more. If you do decide to take a Sunday drive, easy access to the 101 freeway is less than 1 minute away.

The Lazy Bay is a pet friendly house, equipped with small doggie door going out to backyard.

About the Owners

We brought the house at the end of 2012. After spending vacations in Ventura we fell in love with the area. The beach lifestyle, surfing, kite boarding and diving are what make Ventura an amazing place to live and visit! The relaxing and friendly vibe of the Pierpont neighborhood is what sets it apart from all others.

- Sarah & Andy
